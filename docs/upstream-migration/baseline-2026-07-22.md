## Functional assertions

- Search renders on the three representative pages and uses the `搜尋` label.
- Desktop typing, paste, delete, and Chinese IME composition all refresh search results.
- A search result’s `hl` link scrolls to and temporarily highlights the matched passage.
- The custom `audio` and `site-url` shortcodes render in all existing content.
- Theme toggle, sticky header, table of contents, service worker, and audio auto-play
  retain their current behaviour.
